FUSE VOID

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 2027

1. Data Collection & Cloud Processing

FUSE VOID STEM-X is designed with a "Stateless Architecture". When you process an audio file using our App:

  • Your audio file is temporarily uploaded to our secure edge servers (Cloudflare R2) solely for the purpose of AI separation.
  • No Permanent Storage: Once the separation is complete, the resulting stems are available via a secure, time-limited link that expires in exactly 10 minutes.
  • After the link expires, all associated audio files (both input and output) are permanently deleted from our servers. We do not keep copies of your music.

2. Personal Information

We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal identifiable information (PII). The app functions entirely anonymously.

3. Payments & Apple StoreKit

All transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store (StoreKit). We do not collect, process, or store your credit card information or billing details. We only receive a cryptographic receipt (JWS) from Apple to verify that a purchase was made in order to allocate processing time.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any third-party tracking SDKs (like Facebook Pixel or Google Analytics) inside the macOS/iOS app that track your behavior across other apps or websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security of your data, please contact our support team at: support@fusevoid.com
